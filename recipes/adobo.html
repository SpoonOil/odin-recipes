Meat Adobo

Description

Adobo is a versatile and tasty dish that can be used to make any meat into a delicious meal. Its super simple to execute, and takes very few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Meat of your choice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar

Directions

  1. Mince the garlic
  2. Dump all the ingredients in a pot and bring it to a boil
  3. As it fully reduced, keep refilling with more water until its at desired doneness
  4. When its of a desired doneness, let it reduce to a glaze
