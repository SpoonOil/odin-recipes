Meatballs

Description

This meatball recipe gives you a delicious soft meatball with a nice seared crust. The size can vary as preferred , but the cooking time must be adjusted accordingly. For best results, serve with a side of your choice or even a sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt
  • Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Form into balls
  3. Sear in a pan
  4. Bake
  5. Serve.
